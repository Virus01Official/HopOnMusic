About Hopon Music

Welcome to Hopon Music, your go-to platform for uploading, managing, and enjoying your favorite music!

Features

  • Upload Your Songs: Share your favorite songs with the world by uploading MP3 files. Easily add song titles, artists, and even upload album covers.
  • Play Your Music: After uploading, you can instantly listen to your songs and enjoy your collection of music.
  • Manage Your Songs: Edit song details, upload new thumbnails, or delete songs you no longer want to keep.
  • User Roles: We have different roles, including User and Moderator, each with specific permissions. Moderators can manage user submissions and moderate content.
  • Collaborate with Others: Connect with other users through your shared music collection, discover new songs, and explore the music library!

How to Use the App

  1. Sign Up: Create an account to get started. Choose your role as either a regular user or, if you’re an admin, as a moderator.
  2. Upload Songs: Navigate to the upload page and choose MP3 files to upload. Optionally, you can add a song thumbnail.
  3. Play Your Music: Visit your music collection to listen to your uploaded songs anytime!
  4. Edit or Delete: You can edit the song details or remove songs that you no longer wish to keep.
  5. Manage Content (For Moderators): If you're a moderator, you have the ability to approve, edit, or remove songs uploaded by other users.
